Requirements:

Take coordinates of buildings affected.
Retrieve pre- and post-conflict satellite imagery (Google Earth/Maps).
Estimate surface area destroyed from those images.
From Street View, estimate number of floors (≈3 m per floor).
Combine them → compute total debris mass per building.
(Later) estimate material composition via contextual or auxiliary data.


Notes:

Google images may be outdated, might need to instead use NASA EarthData, Maxar Open Data, or Sentinel Hub APIs
Google street view coverage may be limited in Lebanon, might need to instead use OpenStreetMap data, LIDAR, or manual sun/shadow calculation to estimate building height / number of floors
Identifying damage area uses pixel change in pre/post imagery, might be inefficient, benefits from an ML model rather than algorithmic calculation
    can optionally go by a collapsed/non-collapsed state, i.e. all pre- surface area is damaged
Volume and Mass calculations are direct computations from previous data
material distribution will later be covered
